About Me

Hi! I'm Atanaz Khandan-Barani (she/her), a Vancouver-based artist diving into the exciting world of graphic design. While my roots lie in photography, I've had the opportunity to explore and create within multiple disciplines, but more recently with a special focus on graphic design. Earning a Bachelor's degree in Fine Arts with a major in Photography from Emily Carr University of Arts & Design and backed by a certificate in graphic design from the British Columbia Institute of Technology, I'm equipped with a diverse skill set in Adobe Creative Suite, web design, visual composition, social media content creation and a passion for storytelling through visuals. I have a passion for package design, branding, and advertisement design, but I am always up for a creative challenge. My design style is very eclectic and flexible, I enjoy tangible inspiration; whether that be old magazines I've found in a thrift store or stones I've picked up from the beach, I can find inspirational value in everything!
